How It Works

From your imagination to a delicious creation in three simple steps.

1. Design Online

Use our interactive platform to choose your shape, flavor, and intricate designs. Upload your own image or logo for a truly personal touch.

2. We Print with Precision

Our cutting-edge 3D food printers bring your design to life with unparalleled accuracy, minimizing waste and ensuring perfection.

3. Enjoy Your Treat

Pick up your custom creation from our Toronto store or have it delivered for your special occasion. It's that simple!
