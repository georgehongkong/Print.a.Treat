Baking the Future

We're an innovative bakery specializing in fully customizable 3D-printed cakes and pastries, combining advanced technology with the timeless art of baking.

Where Technology Meets Taste

Print-a-Treat Creation was born from a simple idea: what if your imagination was the only limit to a dessert's design? We set out to revolutionize the baking industry by merging cutting-edge 3D printing technology with the craft of high-end patisseries. This allows us to create intricate, precise, and stunning designs that were once impossible.

From our flagship store in downtown Toronto, we're not just making cakes; we're crafting edible art that tells a story, celebrates moments, and brings joy to our community.

Our Mission

"To produce personalized, sustainable, and memorable desserts for every occasion, emphasizing exceptional customer service, innovative design, and environmentally responsible practices. We aim to surpass customer expectations with tailor-made solutions that celebrate creativity while minimizing waste and supporting our local community."

What We Stand For

Innovation & Creativity

We leverage state-of-the-art 3D printing to push the boundaries of dessert design, offering unparalleled personalization and artistic freedom.

Sustainability

Our process is designed to be eco-friendly by precisely using ingredients to minimize food waste. We're proud to source locally whenever possible.

Exceptional Quality

From the finest local ingredients to our meticulous design review process, we are committed to delivering a product that is as delicious as it is beautiful.
